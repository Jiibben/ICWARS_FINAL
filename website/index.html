The shop is the place where you can buy items to boost your units and win the fight

The items avaible for sale are :

  • La BIERE PG : augmente l'attaque de 1.
  • Une potion : soigne 3 points de vie.

Pour ouvrir le shop il faut appuyer sur la touche s en étant sur une unité. You earn money by killing other units or by capturing new cities.

Notre jeu possède 4 unités jouables

Les unités jouables sont :

  • Les tanks
  • Les soldats
  • Les bateaux
  • Les geeks

chacune de ces unités possède un différent set d'action parmis :

  • Patch
  • Hack
  • Capture
  • Attack
  • Wait

In our game there are 7 maps you can play each of them have their own set of cells

  • The maps all have a different set of units you can play with
  • There is an aquatic map with boats on it
  • There is a map with 23 tanks
  • At level 3 each map has cities you can conquer to earn some war spoils.

Don't hesitate to challenge yourself with the most difficult ones.

In our game there are city that you can capture:

To capture a city your unit must be placed on top of it and use its capture action that will change the look of the city to match your faction.

They are one of the best way to win gold so you can buy item in the shop.

  • Capture !
  • Earn money !
  • Win the game !
