The shop is the place where you can buy items that boost your units

The items avaible for sales are :

  • BIERE PG : augmente l'attaque de 1.
  • POTION : soigne 3 points de vie.

To open the shop you must press S while on a unit. You can earn money by killing enemy units or by capturing cities.

Our game has 4 playable units

They are :

  • TANKS
  • SOLDIERS
  • BOAT
  • GEEKS

Each of these units have some of the following actions:

  • Patch
  • Hack
  • Capture
  • Attack
  • Wait

In our game there are 7 maps you can play each of them has a new layout

  • Different units avaible to you
  • An aquatic map !
  • 23 tanks on the famous EPFL MAP
  • from level 3 to 5 each map has cities you can capture and earn war spoils.

In our game there are city that you can capture:

To capture a city your unit must be placed on it and use the capture action, the city will change sprite the city to match your faction color.

They are one of the best way to win money so you can spend it all on shop items.
